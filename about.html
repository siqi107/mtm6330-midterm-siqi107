Our Story

We are four best friends since childhood. Four lovely girls like us gather together for a small business which we are enthusiastic about. We give our baking studio a cute name-Magic Baking Factory. Our goal is to show the magic of backing to children and their parents, and teach them how to bake. They can take part in the baking course which is hold every week, and buy various homemade fresh cookies, cakes, breads at any time of the day.
