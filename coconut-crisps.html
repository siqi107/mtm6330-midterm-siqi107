Coconut Crisps

$ 3

Preheat oven to 325 degrees.Cream sugar and butter; add eggs one at a time beating well after each addition. Add vanilla. Sift flour, baking powder and salt into mixture. Combine cornflakes, coconut, and nuts. Gently fold this into batter. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto a greased baking sheet. Bake for 15 minutes until golden.
