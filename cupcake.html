Cupcake

A cupcake is a small cake designed to serve one person, which may be baked in a small thin paper or aluminum cup. As with larger cakes, icing and other cake decorations such as fruit and candy may be applied.A standard cupcake uses the same basic ingredients as standard-sized cakes.

Cupcakes may be topped with frosting or other cake decorations. They may be filled with frosting, fruit, or pastry cream.
